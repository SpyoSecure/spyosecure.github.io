Category: Defacement | Author: SpyoSecure | July 6, 2025 | 6:34 PM
The hacktivist group Liwaa Mohammad, also known as the Muhammad Brigade, has claimed responsibility for the defacement of the official website of TAMAM Company, a Saudi firm focused on building maintenance and technical services.

Currently, accessing tamam-c.sa redirects users to a suspended page showing the message: "This Account has been suspended."
