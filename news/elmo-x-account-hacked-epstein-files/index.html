Elmo's X account hacked to spread antisemitic content and Epstein demands

Category: Account Hack | Author: SpyoSecure | July 14, 2025 | 9:05 AM
On July 13, 2025, the official X (formerly Twitter) account of Elmo was compromised by an unknown attacker. The account began posting offensive and antisemitic content, including a direct challenge to U.S. President Donald Trump to “release the files” referring to the alleged Jeffrey Epstein client list.

Hackmanac Cyber News was among the first to report the breach via their Telegram channel. The messages, which spread quickly, sparked backlash before they were swiftly deleted.

FoxLA confirmed the incident, showing screenshots of the deleted posts and reporting that Sesame Workshop acknowledged the breach. They also confirmed that all unauthorized content has been removed.

SpyoSecure can independently confirm that the posts are no longer visible on Elmo’s X timeline at this time.
