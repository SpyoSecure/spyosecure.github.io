RuskiNet leaks personal data from Weisslaw.co.il

Category: Leak | Author: SpyoSecure | July 5, 2025 | 11:07 PM
The pro-Palestinian hacker group RuskiNet has published what they claim to be over 3,000 lines of leaked personal data from the Israeli legal site Weisslaw.co.il.

The dataset reportedly includes names, IP addresses, email addresses, home addresses, and phone numbers of individuals connected to the site.

The leak was shared on the underground hacking forum DarkForum by a user going by the name YK3, who is believed to be a member of RuskiNet. No technical details were provided about how the breach occurred.
