Keymous+ allegedly takes down Microsoft.com in unexplained DDoS attack

Category: Attack | Author: SpyoSecure | June 30, 2025 | 7:06 PM
The hacker group Keymous+ has claimed responsibility for a Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attack on Microsoft.com.

The group shared a brief message on their Telegram channel with no context, simply saying "Hmmmm". The attached screenshot showed Microsoft’s homepage displaying the message: “Thank you for your patience. We are currently experiencing high demand. Please wait and try again later.”

Monitoring tools at the time of the post reported connection timeouts, suggesting Microsoft’s website may have been affected. However, the company has made no official statement.

As of now, Microsoft.com appears to be functioning normally.
