Inteid Claims Multiple Amazon Website Outages

Category: Attack | Author: SpyoSecure | July 6, 2025 | 7:21 PM
The hacker group Inteid has claimed responsibility for a series of Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks targeting Amazon's regional websites.

On July 2, Inteid announced that they had taken down amazon.de, the German branch of Amazon.
On July 6, the group claimed responsibility for outages on both amazon.co.jp (Japan) and amazon.com.br (Brazil).

No official reason behind the attacks have been released by Inteid, and Amazon has not yet commented on the incidents.
